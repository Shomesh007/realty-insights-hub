# Content Strategy for Dubai Real Estate SEO

## Goals
- Rank for "Dubai real estate", "lykaconnect", "lykarealty", and related keywords
- Attract investors, buyers, and NRI audiences
- Build authority and trust in Dubai property market

## Content Types
- Blog posts: Market trends, investment guides, visa info, area spotlights
- Case studies: Success stories, ROI breakdowns
- Guides: Step-by-step buying, Golden Visa, rental yields
- News: Policy updates, infrastructure changes
- AI-powered analysis: Data-driven forecasts, risk calculators

## Keyword Targets
- Dubai real estate
- Dubai property investment
- Dubai rental yields
- Dubai Golden Visa
- Dubai property appreciation
- LykaConnect, LykaRealty
- NRI Dubai property
- Business Bay, Dubai Marina, Downtown Dubai, Dubai South

## Publishing Cadence
- 2 blog posts per week
- Monthly market report
- Quarterly investment guide

## Internal Linking
- Link blog posts to main service pages
- Cross-link area guides and investment guides
- Use keyword-rich anchor text

## SEO Best Practices
- Use target keywords in titles, meta descriptions, headers
- Add alt text to all images
- Use structured data (schema.org)
- Include canonical URLs
- Optimize for mobile and speed

## Example Blog Titles
- "Dubai Marina Rental Yields: 2025 Forecast"
- "Golden Visa Guide for Tamil Investors"
- "Business Bay Growth Corridor: Investment Opportunities"
- "AI in Dubai Real Estate: How LykaConnect Predicts ROI"
- "Top Mistakes NRI Investors Make in Dubai Property"

## Promotion
- Share posts on LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook
- Collaborate with Dubai real estate influencers
- Guest posts on property portals

## Measurement
- Track rankings for target keywords
- Monitor organic traffic and conversions
- Adjust strategy based on analytics
